---
title: Toum
category: Condiments
---

## Ingredients

- 3 C neutral flavoured oil (canola or safflower)
- 1 C unchopped garlic
- 1/2 C lemon juice
- 2 tsp salt

## Method

- Add salt and garlic to food processor. Mince. 
- With food processor running, slowly pour in some oil. Scrape down bowl. Continue with a few more tablespoons of oil.
- Next, blend while slowly alternatively adding oil, then a bit of lemon juice until both are done. The toum will get fluffy and white.
- Transfer to glass container. Keep in fridge covered with paper towel overnight. Then put air tight lid on. 
- Keep in fridge for up to 3 months.
